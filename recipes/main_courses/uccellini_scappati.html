Uccellini Scappati

Uccellini scappati in Italian, means "escaped birds" in English. Though its name refers an avian animal, the recipe is all about tender pork.

The Uccellini scappati are prepared by rolling up a slice of loin with a slice of bacon and a sage leaf inside and securing everything with a skewer. A few minutes of cooking in the pan, browning the meat in butter and the dish is served!

List of Ingredients:

  • 12 slices pork loin (1 lb.)
  • 1/2 OZ. of thinly sliced pancetta or bacon
  • Unsalted butter
  • Pepper
  • 4 OZ. of pancetta (slab bacon)
  • Sage
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cut pancetta (bacon slab) into 1/4" thick cubes.
  2. On each pork loin slice, layer sliced pancetta and a sage leaf. Roll to close.
  3. Prepare 4 skewers: Add 3 rolls per skewer, alternating with bacon cubes. Melt 1-2 Tbsp. butter in a pan until foamy. Add skewers and brown on all sides. Season with salt and pepper. Serve.
