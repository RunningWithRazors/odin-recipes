Torta Caprese

Torta Caprese, the famous flourless chocolate and almond cake from the island of Capri, is hard not to love. A few essential ingredients are all you need: chocolate, almond flour, butter, sugar and eggs.

List of Ingredients:

  • 1 1/3 STICK of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 CUP of gluten free powdered sugar
  • 1 CUP of almond flour
  • 4 of large eggs
  • Potato starch
  • 1/3 LB. of gluten free dark chocolate, grated
  • 3/4 CUP of almonds, unblanched
  • 1 TSP. of gluten free baking powder
  • 1/2 vanilla bean pod
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F/176°C.
  2. Grease a 9” round pie dish with butter and dust with potato starch.
  3. In a blender, briefly pulse almonds with almond flour.
  4. Cream softened butter with powdered sugar and a pinch of salt until slightly fluffy.
  5. Combine egg yolks with butter, blended almonds, grated chocolate, baking powder, and vanilla bean pod seeds.
  6. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and gently fold into the batter.
  7. Pour the batter into the pie dish and bake for around 45 minutes. Remove and let cool.
  8. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.
