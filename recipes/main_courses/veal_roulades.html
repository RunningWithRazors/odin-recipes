Pancetta-Wrapped Veal Roulades

Roulades or involtini are a timeless classic in Italian cooking. Pancetta-wrapped veal roulades with a sausage and cheese stuffing are easy to prepare and flavor-packed. Round out the meal with saut√©ed zucchini.

This recipe will require 45-60 minutes of your time and serves 4 people.

List of Ingredients:

  • 12 SLICES of veal round
  • 3 OZ. (85 grams) of sausage
  • 4 TBSP. of Grana Padano, grated
  • 12 SLICES of flat pancetta
  • 3 TBSP. of thyme, minced
  • 3 TBSP. of mint, minced
  • Unsalted butter
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Pepper
  • 4 OZ. (115 grams) of baby zucchini
  • 1/4 CUP of stale bread
  • 12 of Fontina or Taleggio cubes
  • Dry white wine
  • Milk
  • Garlic
  • 1/4 CUP of beef broth
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Soak the bread in the milk. Remove the sausage casing and crumble the sausage. Pound the veal slices with a meat tenderizer and trim them. Finely chop 1 1/2 oz. of the veal trimmings and add them to a bowl with the sausage, soaked bread, Grana Padano cheese, salt and pepper. Mix well to make a soft stuffing.
  2. Distribute the stuffing evenly onto the veal slices and place a fontina cube in the middle of each one. Beginning at the narrower end of 1 veal slice, roll up, enclosing the stuffing. Sprinkle with minced thyme. Wrap with a pancetta slice and secure with a toothpick. Repeat with the remaining veal slices.
  3. Slice the zucchini and brown them in a pan with 4 Tbsp. oil and a garlic clove; after 5 minutes, remove from heat and add the minced mint and a pinch of salt.
  4. Warm the broth over low heat. Heat 2 Tbsp. oil and a pat of butter in a large skillet over high heat. Add the veal roulades and brown on all sides for around 10 minutes. Add 1 cup wine and reduce the heat to minimum. Let the wine reduce by 3/4. Add the warm broth and a pat of butter. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with the zucchini.
