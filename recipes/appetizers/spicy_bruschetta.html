Spicy Bruschetta

Bruschetta is a snack that is difficult to give up. Its value lies in the simplicity of its preparation, which sees its success in the most typical products of Italy.

Fresh chili pepper, extra-virgin olive oil, lemon juice, salt and a few slices of stale bread is all that you'll need for this simple spicy bruschetta.

List of Ingredients:

  • 8 slices slightly stale bread;
  • Lemon;
  • Salt;
  • Fresh red chili pepper;
  • Extra-virgin olive oil;

Steps:

  1. Season the slices of bread with extra-virgin olive oil, a pinch of salt, a little bit of chopped chili pepper and a few drops of lemon juice. Serve.
