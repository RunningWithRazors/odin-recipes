Seafood Salad

Italy's classic light, bright salad of gently cooked and marinated seafood with lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, and subtle spices.

Seafood salad is, at its heart, incredibly simple. The seafood should be fresh and tender, the dressing bright and flavorful.

This recipe will require two hours of your time and serves 12 people.

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 LB. / 900 grams of fresh octopus;
  • 1lb 8 OZ. / 8 kg. of mussels, cleaned;
  • 10 OZ. / 280 grams of shrimp tails;
  • Lemon;
  • Dry white wine;
  • Salt;
  • 2 LB. / 900 grams of smooth Venus clams (Callista chione), cleaned;
  • 1 LB. / 450grams of cuttlefish;
  • Garlic;
  • Parsley, chopped;
  • Extravirgin olive oil;
  • Pepper;

Steps:

  1. To make seafood salad, first immerse the octopus in a pot of cold water, then bring to the boil and cook for 50 minutes, then let cool in its cooking water. Place the clams in a pot with 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup white wine, and a clove of garlic. Bring to the boil, replace the lid, reduce the heat and cook for 3 minutes.
  2. Place the mussels in a pot with 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup wine and a clove of garlic, bring to the boil, cover, and cook for 1 minute until they open. Cook the shrimp tails in boiling water for 1-2 minutes, then remove.
  3. Clean the cuttlefish, separating the bodies from the tentacles, then transfer to a saucepan of cold water and bring to the boil. After 7 minutes, remove the bodies and cook the tentacles for a further 5 minutes. Remove the clams and mussels from their shells, chop the cuttlefish, octopus, and shrimp, then toss together in a bowl to combine. Dress the seafood salad with salt, pepper, oil, parsley, and lemon.
